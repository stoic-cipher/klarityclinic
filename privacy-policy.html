7. Your Rights Under HIPAA

  • Access: Request a copy of your PHI in electronic or paper form.
  • Amendment: Ask us to correct inaccurate or incomplete PHI.
  • Accounting: Receive a list of disclosures of your PHI outside treatment, payment, and healthcare operations.
  • Restriction: Request limits on uses or disclosures of your PHI.
  • Confidential Communications: Ask for alternative contact methods or locations (e.g., only via email or at a different address).
  • Complaint: File a complaint with us or the U.S. Department of Health and Human Services if you believe your rights have been violated.
